Blending trend-forward forms with striking fabrics and finishes, these exclusive products bring bold style to any room. Arrange modern armchairs and tufted sofas around a coffee table for chic seating. Fit a circular nesting table between seats and, for a mod look, contrast clean-lined decor with the curvilinear furniture. Rest a geometric vase on the curved table, or display a table lamp with a striking silhouette here. An overhead chandelier brings on-trend elegance to the space. Think mixed-material lighting that incorporates exposed glass bulbs and brassy metallics, or opt for darker metals for a dash of industrial flair.
